About Me

My programming experience comes from a blend of online courses like Bloc and Codecademy, working in C# and GML for game projects in Unity and GameMaker Studio, and automating processes through macros and logic at my last few jobs. I've worked with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, C#, GML, SQL, and VBA macros, and a lot of my focus has been on improving existing processes to make them more efficient, which is one of my passions. In my free time, I typically can be found reading a book, working on the design for my next game, or spending time with my wife and toddler. I recently ran a successful Kickstarter campaign for my party trivia game, Standing Out, and have released it to the public.

In my past few jobs, I've worked cross-functionally with multiple departments in order to improve our processes and increase communication within the companies. I enjoy this type of collaboration within a position because I believe it brings a company together as a single unit across multiple teams.
